Community Rules

  • No griefing: Respect other players' builds and space.
  • No cheating or exploiting: Any form of unfair advantage will result in a ban.
  • Be respectful: No hate speech, harassment, or discrimination of any kind.
  • Keep chat clean: No spamming, advertising, or NSFW content.
  • Follow staff directions: Admins and moderators are here to help — respect their judgment.
  • No hacked clients: Only approved client-side mods are allowed.
  • Use common sense: If it feels wrong, it probably is. Play fair and be kind.

What Happens If You Break the Rules?

  • First Offense: Warning or short mute depending on severity.
  • Second Offense: Temporary ban or longer mute.
  • Severe or Repeated Violations: Permanent ban and removal of content if necessary.

Reporting Players

  • In-Game: Use /report or message a staff member directly.
  • On Discord: Submit a report in the appropriate channel or DM a moderator.
  • Provide Evidence: Screenshots or video help us act quickly and fairly.

Community Standards vs. Game Mechanics

  • Respect over Loopholes: Just because something is technically allowed doesn’t mean it’s in the spirit of fair play.
  • Balance Matters: Exploiting unbalanced systems can still result in punishment.
  • Think Bigger: We're building a world, not just playing a game — community always comes first.
