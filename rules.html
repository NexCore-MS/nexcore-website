Server Rules

Please read our rules carefully to ensure a fun and fair environment for everyone on NexCore. Failure to comply may result in warnings, mutes, kicks, or bans.

General Conduct

  • Be respectful to all players and staff members. No harassment, discrimination, or hate speech.
  • No excessive swearing, spamming, or use of all caps in chat.
  • Do not impersonate staff members or other players.
  • Keep chat in English primarily, unless specified otherwise in certain channels.
  • No advertising other servers, websites, or services without permission.

Gameplay Rules

  • No cheating, hacking, or using unapproved client modifications (e.g., X-Ray, KillAura). Optifine, minimaps (without player/entity radar), and aesthetic mods are generally okay. Ask if unsure.
  • No exploiting glitches or bugs. Report them to staff immediately.
  • Griefing and raiding are part of Survival, but excessive destruction or targeting new players may be moderated. (Be specific about your server's stance here).
  • No building inappropriate structures.
  • AFK farming/machines are allowed within reason. Staff may remove excessively laggy machines. (Specify your server's policy).

Player vs. Player (PvP)

  • Specify PvP rules: Is it enabled everywhere? Only certain zones? Consensual?
  • No combat logging.
  • No excessive spawn camping.

Reporting & Staff Interaction

  • Report rule-breakers to staff members with proof (screenshots, videos).
  • Do not argue with staff decisions in public chat. If you have an issue, discuss it privately or submit an appeal if a system is in place.
  • Staff members have the final say in all rule interpretations and enforcement.

These rules are subject to change. Please check back regularly. Ignorance of the rules is not an excuse.
