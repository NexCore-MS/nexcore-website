Getting Started on NexCore

Welcome to NexCore! This guide will help you begin your adventure.

Starting Your Adventure

Start your survival adventure by teleporting to the Survival World. Gather resources, sell them in the shop, and build your base!

Teleport To Survival World: You can enter the survival world by talking to the "Worlds NPC" (Non-Player Character) at spawn, or by entering the command /rtp (Random Teleport).

A Great Start: Collect useful rewards from Voting or from the Rewards menu (/rewards) for free on the server! Use any crate keys you get in the Crate Area (/warp crates).
